About Me

My name is Brandi Whitmore. I am a fourth year student at the University of Missouri. I plan on attending college for five years. At the end of the five years, I plan on obtaining four certificates and a degree in Information Technology. Growing up I always liked video games and wanted to learn how they worked. I knew from a young age that I wanted to get a job in a tech related field. I still really enjoy playing video games when I can.
I am a proud pet owner of two loving and playful cats. I have always been creative and have picked up several different hobbies. I really enjoy music. I can and enjoy playing several instruments, singing, and dancing. I enjoy crafting different items as well. My most recent project has been turning charms into cute earrings. At one point, I even made coasters out of resin.
